About Tianyi Intelligence
Shenzhen Tianyi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., established in December 2013, specializes in the research, design, production, sales, and service of precision factory automation parts (such as pneumatic components, rolling functional components, high-precision adjustment platforms, four-axis robots, aluminum profiles, industrial robots, industrial control modules, transmitters, and sensors), fixtures and test fixtures, and automation equipment (lines). Integrating technological innovation, the company's factory automation precision standard parts business helps customers realize value through its products, enabling more automated industries and better-quality production workplaces. Based on application scenarios, the company's tooling and test fixtures and automation equipment business currently focuses on two major application areas: consumer electronics (such as smartphones and smart wearables), as well as automotive electronics. The company plans to gradually expand its capabilities to serve more applications in factory automation. With a strategic focus on precision factory automation parts and intelligent equipment, the company aims to achieve rapid growth and become a leading brand in the industry, contributing to China's industrial upgrade and the realization of the Made in China 2025 and Industry 4.0 blueprints.
